About Us

Rovin James Nalica

Rovin grew up in a small town, where life moved as slowly as the mornings without coffee. Raised by his grandmother, a humble baker, Rovin learned early that food and drink weren’t just sustenance—they were love. Inspired by her legacy, Rovin created CozyCup Café as a haven where everyone could find comfort, connection, and the perfect brew. With determination and hard work, Rovin transformed his passion into a community-centered coffee shop that celebrates tradition and innovation.

Teodore James Ponce de Leon

Rovin’s lifelong friend and business-savvy partner, always had an eye for opportunity. From their college days, where Rovin was known as the “coffee guy,” to TJ’s ability to turn any idea into gold, the duo was unstoppable. TJ became CozyCup Café’s first investor and co-founder, bringing a sharp business sense that helped transform Rovin’s dream into reality. For TJ, it wasn’t just about the business; it was about community, and CozyCup became his way of contributing to something meaningful.
